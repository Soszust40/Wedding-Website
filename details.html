Frequently Asked Questions

When do I have to RSVP by?

August 11th, 2026

What happens if I don’t RSVP on time?

You will automatically be marked as a “no” after our deadline. We need a guest headcount for our vendors so please RSVP as soon as you can!

What is the dress code?

We ask that you wear semi-formal to formal attire such as mid to floor length dresses, dressy jumpsuits, and suit and ties. The ceremony will be held outdoors on grass so please take this into consideration. Since we are next to Lake Manistique it can get a little chilly so a shawl or cardigan might be a good idea to have on you.

What will the wedding party be wearing?

The wedding party will be wearing mocha colored dress. Please choose colors that differ from the bridal party.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony will start at 4pm ET. We recommend getting there around 3:00 to 3:30pm to park and get into the venue with plenty of time.

Can I take pictures?

We are having an unplugged ceremony and kindly ask that you keep your cell phones in your purses or pockets during the full ceremony length. We have professional photos being taken throughout the day and would hate for your phone to be in photos. We will share photos of our ceremony once we get them.

Does the venue have parking?

The venue has a lot for parking out front. However, we encourage carpooling if you are able.

Can I bring my kids?

We are sorry but we are unable to accommodate children at our wedding. With the limited seating we are only able accommodate the invited guests.
